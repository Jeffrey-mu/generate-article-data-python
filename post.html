Monoprice True Wireless Plus Earbuds Review

The Monoprice True Wireless Plus Earbuds are an affordable option for those seeking the convenience of wireless earbuds without breaking the bank. These earbuds offer a decent sound quality and a comfortable fit at a fraction of the cost of some of the more well-known brands.

Design and Fit

The Monoprice Earbuds have a sleek and modern design that is similar to many other wireless earbuds on the market. They come with three different sizes of ear tips to ensure a comfortable fit for all users. The earbuds are also sweat-resistant, making them a great option for those who like to work out or participate in outdoor activities.

Connectivity and Controls

The earbuds connect to your device via Bluetooth 5.0, which provides a stable and strong connection. They also come with touch controls that allow you to play, pause, skip tracks, and adjust the volume. The touch controls are easy to use and responsive.

Sound Quality

The Monoprice Earbuds have a decent sound quality, especially considering their price point. The bass is clear and punchy, and the mids and highs are well balanced. The earbuds also have noise isolation, which helps to block out external noise and improve the overall listening experience.

Battery Life

The Monoprice Earbuds have a battery life of up to 4 hours on a single charge. The charging case provides an additional 12 hours of battery life, allowing you to use the earbuds for a full day without needing to recharge.

Conclusion

Overall, the Monoprice True Wireless Plus Earbuds are a great option for those who are looking for affordable wireless earbuds. They offer a comfortable fit, decent sound quality, and a long battery life. While they may not have all of the features of some of the more expensive brands, they are a solid choice for those on a budget.
